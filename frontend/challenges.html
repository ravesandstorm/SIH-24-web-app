Challenges in Managing Power Load

The power load curve in Delhi is highly irregular, largely due to the city's predominant domestic and commercial load, with minimal industrial load. This makes it challenging to maintain a stable load profile, unlike other states where industrial and agricultural loads help stabilize the overall power demand.
