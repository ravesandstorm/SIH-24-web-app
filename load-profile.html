Load Profile of Delhi

The load profile in the NCT of Delhi is highly unique due to significant variations between summer and winter, and even within the same day. The peak load in Delhi can reach up to 8300 MW during the summer, while it drops as low as 2000 MW during winter. Additionally, the city experiences two peaks during summer: one in the afternoon and another late at night.
